Tomato Pasta

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta of choice
  • 1 can Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil leaves

Directions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté minced garlic in olive oil in a pan until fragrant.
  3. Add canned tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes on low heat.
  4. Drain pasta and toss it directly into the tomato sauce.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.

Garden Salad

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 head Lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1 Cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice

Directions

  1. Wash and thoroughly dry lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumber.
  2. Chop the lettuce and place it in a large salad bowl.
  3. Add the halved cherry tomatoes and sliced cucumber.
  4. Drizzle olive oil and fresh lemon juice over the top.
  5. Toss gently to combine and serve immediately.

Banana Pancakes

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe Bananas
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 1 tbsp Butter

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until smooth.
  2. Whisk in the eggs, flour, and milk until a batter forms.
  3. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour small circles of batter into the pan.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden brown.
